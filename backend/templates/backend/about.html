About Tribepool

Share More, Waste Less

Our Mission

Tribepool is on a mission to revolutionize how communities share resources. We believe in the power of collaborative consumption to create a more sustainable and connected world.

Whether it's a power drill you need for a weekend project or a streaming subscription you only use occasionally, Tribepool makes it easy to borrow, lend, and share within your trusted circles.

FAQs

What can I share on Tribepool?

You can share physical items like tools, sports equipment, and appliances, as well as digital resources like streaming subscriptions and online memberships.

Is Tribepool safe?

We encourage users to share within their trusted communities. However, always exercise caution and use your best judgment when sharing or borrowing.

How do I start a community?

Simply sign up, create a community, and invite your friends, neighbors, or colleagues. You can create multiple communities for different groups!

Is there a fee for using Tribepool?

Tribepool is free to join and use during alpha!

Is there a charge for borrowing items or subscriptions?

Some community members may charge an amount for borrowing their items, but this is up to individual users.
